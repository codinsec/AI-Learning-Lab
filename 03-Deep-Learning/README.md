1. **Hands-on practice:** Write code, don't just read

4. **Production mindset:** Think about inference, not just training

## Common Questions

**Q: Do I need a GPU?**  
A: Helpful but not required for learning. CPU works for small examples.

**Q: How deep should I go into math?**  
A: Understand the concepts intuitively. You don't need to derive formulas.

**Q: Should I memorize architectures?**  
A: No. Understand the principles (attention, convolution) and you can adapt.

**Q: What about other frameworks?**  
A: PyTorch is industry standard. Concepts transfer to other frameworks.
